Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and services

What is the duration of your courses?

Course durations vary depending on the exam and intensity of preparation required. Typically, our courses range from 6 to 12 months. You can find specific duration details on each course page.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, we provide comprehensive study materials including textbooks, practice workbooks, previous years' question papers, and online resources. All materials are regularly updated to reflect the latest exam patterns.

Absolutely! We offer both online and offline classes to suit your preferences. Our online platform provides live classes, recorded sessions, and interactive doubt-solving sessions.

We maintain a consistent success rate of over 95% across all our courses. Our students have secured top ranks in various competitive exams and gained admission to prestigious institutions.

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including installment plans and EMI options. Please contact our counseling team for details about available payment plans for your chosen course.
