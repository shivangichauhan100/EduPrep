Course Related Questions

What is the duration of your courses?

Course durations vary depending on the exam and intensity of preparation required. Typically, our courses range from 6 to 12 months. CUET Preparation is 6 months, CLAT Coaching is 8 months, CAT Mastery is 10 months, and NEET/JEE courses are 12 months long.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, we provide comprehensive study materials including textbooks, practice workbooks, previous years' question papers, and online resources. All materials are regularly updated to reflect the latest exam patterns and syllabus changes.

Can I access classes online?

Absolutely! We offer both online and offline classes to suit your preferences. Our online platform provides live classes, recorded sessions, interactive doubt-solving sessions, and digital study materials accessible from anywhere.

What is your teaching methodology?

We follow a comprehensive teaching methodology that includes concept building, regular practice, doubt resolution, mock tests, and performance analysis. Our approach focuses on building strong fundamentals while developing exam-specific strategies.

Admission Process

What is the admission process?

Our admission process is simple: 1) Choose your desired course, 2) Fill out the enrollment form, 3) Complete the payment process, 4) Attend orientation session. You can complete the entire process online or visit our center for assistance.

Are there any eligibility criteria?

Eligibility varies by course. Generally, students in 11th/12th grade or graduates can enroll in our programs. Specific requirements are mentioned on each course page. We also offer foundation courses for younger students.

Can I change my course after enrollment?

Yes, course changes are possible within the first 15 days of enrollment, subject to availability and payment adjustment. Please contact our admission counselors for assistance with course transfers.

Payment & Fees

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including installment plans and EMI options. You can choose to pay in 2-4 installments depending on the course duration and fee structure. Please contact our counseling team for details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, mobile wallets, and cash payments at our centers. All online transactions are secure and encrypted.

Is there a refund policy?

We have a transparent refund policy. Full refund is available within 7 days of enrollment if classes haven't started. Partial refunds are possible later based on the number of classes attended. Please refer to our refund policy document for details.

Student Support

How can I get doubt resolution support?

We provide 24/7 doubt resolution through multiple channels: live chat on our platform, dedicated WhatsApp groups, email support, and scheduled doubt-solving sessions with faculty. You'll never be stuck with your doubts!

Do you provide career counseling?

Yes, we offer comprehensive career counseling services. Our experienced counselors help students choose the right career path, select appropriate courses, and prepare for various competitive exams based on their interests and capabilities.

What is your success rate?

We maintain a consistent success rate of over 95% across all our courses. Our students have secured top ranks in various competitive exams and gained admission to prestigious institutions across India.
